About this map

Definitions:

Neighborhoods are the space of our everyday lives. They are geographies that host communities of shared interest and identity.

Super neighborhood’s are contiguous groupings of neighborhoods or communities that function as an organizing element to help bridge between neighborhood’s and larger municipally designated geographies such as service areas or city council districts.
